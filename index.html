How to play

  • Move: WASD / Arrow keys (or joystick on mobile)
  • Camera: drag mouse / swipe
  • Interact: walk close to a glowing NPC, press E / ✋
  • Emote: click an emoji, or press 1–6
  • Goal: deliver letters & packages, collect 💎, explore every region!
